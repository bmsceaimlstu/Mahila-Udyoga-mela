About Us

    “Avala Hejje” (Kannada phrase that means “Her Steps”) is a social enterprise with the motto that when women step forward, the society takes a leap. Women have time and again proved to be as smart, as hardworking and as ambitious as men, yet they have often fallen behind. Why? Because they face greater barriers than men.
At Avala Hejje, we have set out to empower women in achieving their dreams. Our various programs enable women of all ages to come together, to learn from each other and to become go-getters. Happy employees are productive employees. Banglorians spend nearly 2-4 hours commuting to work. Due to this, a lot of women hesitate to engage in gainful employers.

Our Beginning

Avala Hejje provides a platform to women who share our dream. Our various programs enable women of all ages to become go-getters, to come together and strive to learn from each other.

Our Mission

We are committed to empower women of Karnataka in achieving their dreams. Our mission is two fold: 1. Empower women with knowledge and techniques to aim high, to develop skills and to achieve their potential leading to personal fulfilment. 2. Build a platform to showcase achievements of 1000+ women who have excelled in their fields; women can interact with, draw inspiration and learn from these living examples of success. 3.It is profitable by hiring surronding women, as they can concentrate on work efficiently without spending their time and energy in commuting.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a nation where women are contributing equally to the National GDP as well as leading the society towards higher Gross National Happiness. Our belief is that when women step forward, the society takes a leap.
